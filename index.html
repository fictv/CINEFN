05 Deep Dive
TRIPLEEIGHT

A full-stack product development engagement: engineered from scratch, manufactured to spec, and launched with cinematic media that redefined the category.

Challenge
REINVENT THE FLUID LINE

The client needed a premium motorsport fluid transfer system to compete against Goodridge — requiring new industrial design, engineering, and a full visual identity to support launch.

Engineering
48 COMPONENTS, ONE VISION

Full CAD modeling in SolidWorks, FEA pressure simulation to 5,000 PSI, GD&T documentation, and DFM review with manufacturing partners across three countries.

Prototype
VALIDATE BEFORE TOOLING

Three rapid prototype iterations using SLA resin and CNC-machined aluminum. Each cycle refined fit, finish, and ergonomics before committing to production tooling.

Manufacturing
FROM PROTOTYPE TO PRODUCTION

First article inspection, tolerance stack analysis, and ongoing quality oversight through the production run. 100% in-spec first shipment delivery.

Content Production
CINEMATIC LAUNCH CAMPAIGN

4K product photography, 60-second commercial, and 12 social content pieces. Shot in a controlled industrial studio environment with cinematic lighting and precision staging.

Results
MARKET ENTRY SUCCESS

Launched to immediate industry recognition. Secured placement with professional racing teams within 90 days of launch with all media assets deployed.

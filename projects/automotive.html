Case Study / Commercial Video

AUTOMOTIVE
MEDIA PRODUCTION

A cinematic automotive production — capturing performance, craft, and identity in a 2-day commercial shoot.

The Challenge

The client needed hero-level automotive content that could anchor a brand relaunch — photography and video that communicated performance, precision, and premium positioning without a Hollywood budget.

Project Breakdown
Brief
DEFINE THE VISION

Creative brief, mood board development, and location scouting to lock the visual direction before production begins.

Pre-Production
PLAN THE SHOOT

Shot list, call sheet, crew scheduling, vehicle prep, and permit coordination for location shooting.

Production Day 1
HERO PHOTOGRAPHY

Full-day photo shoot — static hero shots, detail photography, and rolling shots on location.

Production Day 2
VIDEO PRODUCTION

Full crew video day — cinematic driving footage, interior detail video, and b-roll for commercial and social use.

Post Production
COLOR & EDIT

4K color grading, sound design, music licensing, and delivery in all required formats.

Delivery

Complete asset library delivered — hero images, commercial cut, social cuts, and raw selects archive.
